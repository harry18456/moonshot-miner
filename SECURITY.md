## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take security seriously. If you discover a security vulnerability, please do NOT open an issue.

Instead, please email **harry18456@gmail.com**. You will receive a response within 48 hours.

The team will investigate the report and determine the next steps. We appreciate your efforts to responsibly disclose your findings, and will make every effort to acknowledge your contributions.
